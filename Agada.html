MY TECH JOURNEY SO FAR.

I am Agada Sylvester Okwute from Orokam in Ogbadibo Local Government Area of Benue State, Nigeria .

I completed my primary education from Bishop ameh memorial model nursery and primary school, Otukpo, Benue State.

I also went to Government secondary school Otukpo, later switched to Government day secondary school Otukpo, and finally went to Okam high school Orokam in Ogbadibo local government area of Benue State, where i completed my secondary education with the acquisition of both WAEC and NECO Certificates.

Then I proceed to the Benue state University Makurdi, where i acquired my Bachelor of science degree in Biology.

Then went to the compulsory one year national youth service corps in Osun State, Nigeria.

After my NYSC, I came back and enrolled in the National Open University of Nigeria, where i acquired my masters in education Administration and Planning.

And am currently studying Applied IT Support and Operations Engineer at the New Vision Institute of Technology (NVIT) in Otukpo, Benue State Nigeria to further boost my educational qualifications/ Experience to meet up with the current technological trendsetter.
